In high school I found art and I found Photoshop. I worked hard to move to San Francisco to study New Media and to help me find my voice and express it. I found that there’s much more to life than pretty pictures and began honing my skills in graphic communication, motion graphics and programming. Versatility is key.

Through other jobs and self study I became proficient at these skills and can now adapt to practically any project. My specialty is programming for the web. I love working in a team, I love a challenge, and I love achieving the unexpected.
